💰 Mana System

Wager mana in battles. Win to gain mana, lose to forfeit your wager. Higher stakes, greater rewards!

🛡️ Fair Elemental System

When elements tie, elementals decide the outcome fairly - you never lose more than you wagered:

• Stronger elemental: Gets wager back + wins difference from opponent
• Weaker elemental: Keeps protected portion, loses the rest
• Equal elementals: True draw - full wager returned
• Maximum loss: Never more than your wager!

🏆 Progression

Gain experience through battles, unlock achievements, and climb the ranks from Novice to Immortal Master.
